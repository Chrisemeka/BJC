Publication Overview

Our Mission

We are dedicated to publishing high-quality, peer-reviewed research that advances the fields of computer science, information technology, and software engineering. Our mission is to provide a reputable platform for scholars and practitioners to share innovations, foster academic dialogue, and support technological progress in a rapidly evolving digital world.

Scope

We invite original research and review articles across the core and emerging areas of computing, with a focus on contributions that align with our three specialized journals:

  • Babcock Journal of Information Technology and Security (BJITS): Cybersecurity, data protection, IT governance, information systems, and network security.
  • Journal of Computing and Software Engineering (JCSE): Software development methodologies, software architecture, programming paradigms, software testing, and DevOps.
  • Babcock Journal of Computing and Emerging Technology (BJCET): Artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud computing, blockchain, IoT, and other cutting-edge computing technologies.
