About Babcock Journals of Computing

Mission and Scope

Babcock Journals of Computing (BJC) is a peer-reviewed, open access publication platform committed to disseminating impactful research in the fields of computer science, information technology, and software engineering. Our mission is to support global academic and industry communities by providing access to high-quality, original studies that advance both theoretical understanding and practical application in the digital era.

We welcome original research articles, systematic reviews, technical reports, and perspectives that address current challenges and propose innovative solutions within our focus disciplines. BJC hosts three specialized sub-journals:

Journal Scope

Our publication covers a wide range of areas, structured around the following sub-journals:

  • Babcock Journal of Information Technology and Security (BJITS): Cybersecurity, digital forensics, IT infrastructure, data privacy, and secure systems design.
  • Journal of Computing and Software Engineering (JCSE): Software development life cycle, agile methodologies, system design, software quality assurance, and programming practices.
  • Babcock Journal of Computing and Emerging Technology (BJCET): Artificial intelligence, machine learning, big data analytics, blockchain, quantum computing, and emerging digital innovations.

Editorial Board

Our editorial board consists of leading experts in catalysis science from around the world. The board is responsible for maintaining the high scientific standards of the journal and overseeing the peer review process.
