Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about submission guidelines, publication procedures, and policies for Babcock Journal of Computing (BJC).

Submission Guidelines

Q: Should I use the template for submission?

A: While using our official template is highly recommended, it is not mandatory. The template ensures proper formatting and includes all required sections, which can speed up the review process. If you don't use the template, ensure your manuscript follows our formatting guidelines exactly:

  • Double-spaced, 12-point Times New Roman font
  • 1-inch margins on all sides
  • Line numbers throughout the document
  • Proper heading structure and reference format

Download the template: BJCET Manuscript Template

Q: Is there a word or page limit?

A: Yes, we have the following limits:

  • Research Articles: 8,000-12,000 words (excluding references)
  • Review Articles: 10,000-15,000 words (excluding references)
  • Short Communications: 3,000-5,000 words (excluding references)
  • Technical Notes: 2,000-4,000 words (excluding references)
  • Abstract: 250-300 words maximum
  • Keywords: 5-8 keywords maximum

Note: Manuscripts significantly exceeding these limits may be subject to desk rejection or require substantial revision.

Q: What file formats are accepted for submission?

A: We accept manuscripts in the following formats:

  • Main Document: Microsoft Word (.docx) or LaTeX (.tex with PDF)
  • Figures: High-resolution TIFF, PNG, or EPS (minimum 300 DPI)
  • Tables: Embedded in main document or separate .docx files
  • Supplementary Materials: PDF, Word, Excel, or multimedia files

Q: How do I find out that my article processing charges has been safely received?

A: You will receive confirmation through multiple channels:

  1. Automatic Email Receipt: Instant confirmation for online payments
  2. Editorial Office Confirmation: Within 24-48 hours for bank transfers
  3. Manuscript Status Update: Status changes to "Payment Received" in your submission portal
  4. SMS Notification: If mobile number is provided during submission

Need to verify payment status? Email us at bjcet.editorial@babcock.edu.ng with your manuscript ID and payment reference.

Q: When exactly do I need to pay the APC?

A: APC payment is required only after your manuscript is accepted for publication. The sequence is:

  1. You submit your manuscript (no payment required)
  2. Manuscript undergoes peer review
  3. If accepted, you receive an acceptance letter with invoice
  4. You make the payment within 30 days
  5. Article is published after payment confirmation

Review Process

Q: How long does the review process take?

A: Our typical review timeline is:

  • Initial Editorial Screening: 5-7 business days
  • Peer Review: 6-8 weeks
  • Author Revision: 4-6 weeks (if required)
  • Final Decision: 2-4 weeks after revision
  • Total Time: 10-15 weeks from submission to final decision

Q: Can I check the status of my manuscript?

A: Yes, you can track your manuscript status 24/7 through:

  • Your submission portal dashboard
  • Email notifications at key milestones
  • Direct email inquiry to the editorial office

Common status updates include: Submitted, Under Review, Revision Required, Accepted, Payment Pending, In Production, Published.

Technical Support

Q: I'm having trouble uploading my manuscript. What should I do?

A: Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check file size (maximum 50MB total)
  2. Ensure files are in accepted formats (.docx, .pdf, .tiff, .png)
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  4. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  5. Disable browser extensions/plugins temporarily

If problems persist, contact our technical support at tech.support@babcock.edu.ng
