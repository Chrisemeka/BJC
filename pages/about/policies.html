Journal Policies

Publication Ethics

Catalyst Journals is committed to maintaining the highest standards of publication ethics and takes all possible measures against publication malpractice. Our policies are based on COPE's (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines.

Authorship

All listed authors must have made significant contributions to the research and approved the final version of the manuscript. Any changes to authorship after submission must be approved by all authors.

Originality and Plagiarism

Authors must ensure that their work is entirely original. Any text or ideas borrowed from other sources must be properly cited. Plagiarism in any form constitutes unethical behavior and is unacceptable.

Data Access and Retention

Authors may be asked to provide raw data related to their manuscript for editorial review and should be prepared to make such data publicly available if practicable.

Peer Review Policy

All submissions to Catalyst Journals undergo rigorous peer review based on initial editor screening and anonymous refereeing by at least two expert reviewers.

Review Process

  1. Initial submission check by editorial staff for completeness and journal scope
  2. Editor-in-Chief or Associate Editor assessment for scientific quality
  3. Double-blind peer review by at least two experts
  4. Editorial decision based on reviewer recommendations
  5. Author revision if required
  6. Final acceptance and production

Reviewer Guidelines

Reviewers are expected to provide constructive, objective evaluations of manuscripts within their area of expertise. Reviews should be thorough and timely, maintaining confidentiality throughout the process.

Open Access Policy

Catalyst Journals is a fully open access journal. All articles are immediately available to read and download upon publication, without any subscription or paywall barriers.

License

All published articles are licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License. This license allows others to share and adapt the work, provided proper attribution is given.

Author Rights

Authors retain copyright of their work and are free to deposit all versions of their manuscript in institutional or other repositories.

Copyright Policy

Authors grant Catalyst Journals a license to publish the article and identify itself as the original publisher. Authors retain copyright and full publishing rights without restrictions.

Permissions

No permission is required to reuse published content as long as the original work is properly cited. For commercial reuse, please contact the editorial office.

Preprint Policy

Authors may submit manuscripts that have been posted on preprint servers. The preprint should be cited in the cover letter upon submission.
