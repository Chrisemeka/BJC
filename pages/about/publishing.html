Publishing Procedures

About Our Publishing Process

The Babcock Journal of Computing (BJC) operates under a transparent and efficient publishing process designed to ensure the highest quality of published research. As an open-access journal, we are committed to making cutting-edge research freely available to the global scientific community.

Submission Process

Authors can submit their manuscripts through our online submission portal. The following steps outline our submission process:

  1. Manuscript Preparation: Authors must ensure their manuscripts adhere to BJCET formatting guidelines and include all required sections (abstract, keywords, references, etc.)
  2. Online Submission: Submit through our manuscript management system with all required files (main document, figures, tables, supplementary materials)
  3. Initial Screening: Editorial team conducts initial assessment within 5-7 business days
  4. Plagiarism Check: Automated plagiarism detection using iThenticate software
  5. Editorial Decision: Manuscripts proceed to peer review or receive desk rejection with feedback

Peer Review Process

BJCET employs a rigorous double-blind peer review process to maintain scientific excellence:

  • Reviewer Selection: At least two independent experts are selected based on their expertise in the manuscript's subject area
  • Review Timeline: Standard review period of 6-8 weeks, with regular follow-ups to ensure timely completion
  • Double-Blind Process: Both author and reviewer identities remain anonymous throughout the review process
  • Review Criteria: Reviewers assess originality, technical quality, clarity, significance, and relevance to the journal's scope
  • Decision Categories: Accept, Minor Revisions, Major Revisions, or Reject

Revision and Final Decision

Following peer review, authors receive detailed feedback and recommendations:

  • Reviewer Comments: Comprehensive feedback provided for all revision requests
  • Response Letter: Authors must submit a detailed response letter addressing each reviewer comment
  • Re-review Process: Major revisions undergo re-review by original or new reviewers
  • Final Decision: Editor makes final decision based on revised manuscript and author responses
  • Appeals Process: Authors may appeal negative decisions with additional evidence or clarifications

Publication Timeline

Our typical publication timeline includes the following milestones:

  • Submission to First Decision: 8-12 weeks
  • Revision Period: 4-6 weeks (author-dependent)
  • Final Decision: 2-4 weeks after revision submission
  • Copy Editing: 2-3 weeks
  • Online Publication: Immediate upon final acceptance
  • DOI Assignment: Within 24 hours of publication

What the APC Covers

  • Rigorous peer review process management
  • Professional copy editing and proofreading
  • XML and PDF production services
  • DOI registration and metadata submission
  • Indexing in major databases
  • Hosting and preservation of published content
  • 24/7 online access to readers worldwide

Payment Information

  • When to Pay: APC is due upon acceptance of the manuscript for publication
  • Payment Methods: Bank transfer, credit card (Visa/MasterCard), PayPal
  • Invoice Processing: Invoices are generated within 48 hours of acceptance
  • Publication Hold: Articles will not be published until APC payment is received

Waivers and Discounts

BJCET recognizes the financial constraints faced by researchers from developing countries and institutions with limited resources. We offer:

  • Full Waivers: Available for authors from low-income countries (as defined by World Bank criteria)
  • 50% Discount: Available for authors from lower-middle-income countries
  • Student Discount: 25% discount for first-author students with verification
  • Institutional Agreements: Special rates for subscribing institutions
